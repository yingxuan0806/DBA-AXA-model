JK Cement

India

J.K. Cement Limited manufactures and sells cement and its related products in India. The company offers grey cement products, including ordinary Portland, Portland Pozzolana, and Portland slag cements under the JK Super Cement brand name; cement products for concrete under the JK Super Strong Cement brand; and white cement for floors, walls, and decorative and architectural applications under the JK White Cement brand name. It also provides white cement based primer under the JK Primaxx brand name.
